Agriturismo Dogana

Accommodation

The property sleeps 60 people in a variety of different buildings. The Coach House has 11 apartments of varying sizes with all the amenities you will need for your short stay here. Each apartment has a living room and small kitchenette area as well as a shared space where all guests can come together. At the from of the property, there is a beautiful pool with sun beds sit alongside.

The entire property is part of an olive grove and is next door to a vineyard where we will be getting our wine for the wedding.

Local Area

La Dogana is located beside Lake Trasimeno in the province of Perugia, in the Umbria region of Italy on the border with Tuscany. The venue is close to the lovely towns of Passignano, Cortona and Tuoro sul Trasimeno. These are all easily accesible by car or there are local buses running every hour from the gate of the property

There are three islands on Lake Trasimeno; Maggiore, Minore and Polvese, each of which are accessible by ferry.

Things To Do

You definitely won't be stuck for things to do here in your downtime. There is wine tasting available in the neighbouring vineyard which is only a 15 minute walk away. Horse riding is another option that can be organised by La Dogana in Tuscany across the Cortona hills. There are boat trips each day to the islands on Trasimeno Lake. Magiore Island is the biggest of the three islands with some shops and an old church to visit.

There is a supermarket located 2km from the accomodation and plenty of restaurants and cafes in the neighbouring towns.
