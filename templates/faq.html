FAQs



What is the weather usually like in Italy in September?


It can vary but the staff at La Dogana have assured me it is usually dry and ranges in temperature from 13-23 degrees Celcius


When should I register my preferences by?


We know with Covid and with those of you that have kids, planning this far in advance can be difficult. We just ask that you let us know about 6 months before so March 2022 if you can. Hoping as many of you as possible can come!

Flights tend to go on sale 11 months in advance. Myself and Dave will be keeping an eye on flights and we will keep you all in the loop by adding an update to the site.

Don't worry about the accommodation. It is our way of showing our appreciation for you coming to our big day! We have booked out the entire property for all our guests so it is one less expense for you to worry about!


Are the ceremony and reception in the same place?


Yes, both will be held at La Dogana. They are fully equipped to accommodate weddings up to 100 people. The grounds are absolutely stunning and were exactly what we were looking for!
